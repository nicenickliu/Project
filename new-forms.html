New Crafted Beer Products

Current days, there are nearly 9000 breweries across United States. As the covid becomes less of a problem, the whole crafted beer industry has been through the setback effect and developed new trends throughout the world including Low-ABV, Non-ABV beers, crafted hard selzer, new IPAs and etc.


Tasting crafted beer is nice, but you still need to be careful about how much you are drinking. If you drink to a certain amount, you should not drive! Check if you can still drive by putting in how many 12 ounce beer you have drunk.
