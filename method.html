Brewing Methods


Ale and Lager are the two major methods of brewing. The beer brewed by Ale and Lager are drasticly different.The Industrial beer is usually brewed with lager. The crafted beer usualy goes with Ale

Ale Lager
  • Top-fermenting Yeast
  • Warmth Temperature
  • Richer Taste
  • Darker Color
  • Crafted Brewery Tends to Produce
  • Bottom-fermenting Yeast
  • Cold Temperature
  • Need to Hold Still for Long Time
  • More Thirst-quenching
  • More Expensive to make for Higher Quality
  • Lighter Color
  • Mass Production Tends to Produce
